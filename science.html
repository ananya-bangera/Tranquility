Mental Health is as important as Physical Health

Over the last 15 years, Scientists have come to realize that positive psychology is a serious and legitimate area of study, with a great deal of validated research and studies supporting it.
Here are some of the most important conclusions:




  1. Our Brains Can Change

    That the brain we're born with can be changed. Technically speaking, they call that neuroplasticity. (You can teach an old brain new tricks.)

  2. Adopting New Thought Patterns
    Is Possible

    We can change it by adopting new thought
    patterns, by training our brain as if it were a muscle, to overcome negative thoughts.

  3. Humans Are Hard-Wired for
    Negativity

    All of us are hard-wired for negativity (blame
    evolution!) but can profoundly benefit from learning new ways to react and deal with everyday stresses.

  4. It’s Easier Than You Think

    It doesn't take a lot of effort to make a real difference in your life. A few simple and even entertaining mental diversions done regularly will change things.
